********************************
* Mapping for input characters
*
* When a key is proessed on the keyboard, if it is
* an ANSI sequence, that is in the list, it will
* be translated to a single character 
* 
* IN_MAP_<ascii>=<ansi-sequence>
*
********************************

IN_MAP_$13=^[1~   * HOME
IN_MAP_$94=^[2~   * INSERT
IN_MAP_$7F=^[3~   * DELETE
IN_MAP_$93=^[4~   * END
IN_MAP_$1D=^[C    * RIGHT
IN_MAP_$9D=^[D    * LEFT
